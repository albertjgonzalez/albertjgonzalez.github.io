About me.

My name is Albert Gonzalez and welcome to my online portfolio. Be sure to check out all my work and comeback for regular updates!

Where I've been.

I grew up in the turn of the century, so seeing how technology has dramatically changed the way we live over the past two decades really inspired me to get into the tech field. With a background in HVAC systems, I take my hands-on technical abilities and combine it with natural creative spirit to understand and build the world around me.

Where I'm going.

My over-arching goal is to design software that can be implemented into my current music production and video workflow. I would love to give back to the artist community by building applications that will change how many young creatives work day-to-day.
